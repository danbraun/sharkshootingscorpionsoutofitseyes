Shark Shooting Scorpions Out Of Its Eyes

One day during a Slack conversation, I mentioned a shark shooting scorpions out of its eyes. In an attempt to embellish my statement, I searched Google images for a shark shooting scorpions out of its eyes. I was astonished to find out that an image of this was not available. There ere sharks. There were scorpions. But the internet did not seem to possess an image of a shark shooting scorpions out of its eyes.

The purpose of this webpage is to provide an image of a shark shooting scorpions out of its eyes.
